About Our Project

Our Mission

We are dedicated to improving breast cancer detection through advanced artificial intelligence, making early diagnosis more accessible and accurate.

Our AI-powered system assists healthcare professionals in identifying potential signs of breast cancer in mammogram images, serving as a valuable second opinion in the diagnostic process.

How It Works

1. Upload Image

Upload your mammogram image in supported formats

2. AI Analysis

Our AI model analyzes the image using deep learning

3. Get Results

Receive detailed analysis with confidence scores

Technology

Our system utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning models trained on thousands of mammogram images. The AI model has been developed using:

  • Advanced Convolutional Neural Networks
  • Transfer Learning Techniques
  • High-Performance Computing Infrastructure

Note: While our AI system provides highly accurate results, it is designed to assist, not replace, healthcare professionals in their diagnostic process.
